# Code of conduct

Project spaces must remain professional, inclusive, and focused on technical
collaboration. Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, and
publication of private information are not tolerated.

Maintainers may edit or remove harmful content and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation. Reports should use the repository's
private maintainer or security contact once configured. Good-faith technical
disagreement is welcome; criticism should address behavior and evidence, not
people.
